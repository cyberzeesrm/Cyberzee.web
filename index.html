▼ Click to expand

About the Event

Bytes of Truth was an immersive workshop focused on threat intelligence and Open Source Intelligence (OSINT) methodologies. Participants learned advanced techniques for gathering, analyzing, and leveraging publicly available information to identify and mitigate cyber threats.

The session covered reconnaissance frameworks, social media intelligence, domain and infrastructure analysis, threat actor profiling, and real-world case studies demonstrating how OSINT is used in modern cybersecurity operations.
